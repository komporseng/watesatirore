Who is Princess Love?

Princess Love was born Pilipina Love Norwood in Oakland California on August 14, 1984. Her mother is Filipino, Lena Love while her father, Hugh Love is an African-American.

On an episode of Love and Hip Hop Hollywood her father abandoned her and her siblings when she was still very young. Reports also claim that her parents split up when she was only 5 years old. After her parents divorced, she became very close to her grandmother, Barbara Love.

She attended Texas Southern University and the International Academy of Design and Technology before focusing on her fashion design career.

Her marriage with Ray J and their two kids

In 2014, Princess Love and Ray J started dating. That was after her relationship with famous boxer Floyd Mayweather Jr. ended in May 2014.

Barely a year after the two started dating, they engaged in January 2015. Princess and her then-boyfriend got married on August 12, 2016.

Their marriage has produced two children, daughter Melody Norwood(5) and son Epik Ray Norwood(4).

Divorce Drama of the model and Ray J

Later on, Princess had to clear the air that their alleged reconciliation. She revealed that she and her ex-husband only found a safe way to co-parent their two children but they have also moved on with their personal lives.

Apparently, that was the third time the couple had filed for divorce. Love first filed for divorce in May 2020 shortly after they got into a huge fight in Las Vegas. According to reports, their off-and-on relationship was allegedly due to Ray J’s infidelity.

In addition to that, Love once claimed her estranged ex-husband left her stranded in a Las Vegas hotel while he allegedly hooked up with other women. However, he denied the accusations she made.

While the divorce proceedings were going on, Love revealed in an interview that she was open to making the relationship work if they both agree.

After Ray J Filed for divorce for the third time, he later asked the court to dismiss his petition to divorce after the reconciliation. While celebrating Ray J’s 42nd birthday, the actor and artist declared his plans to start afresh with Princess Love.

Princess Love’s former pattern gained prominence after his sex tape with his then-Kim Kardashian was made public.

Aside from his relationship with Kim, he reportedly had a close relationship with Whitney Houston during the last few years of her life. Although it was speculated that he and Houston had a romantic relationship, Ray J, insisted they were just friends and never dated.
